ChilliCactus Games

Hi, we're ChilliCactus Games, an indie games developer who specialise in mobile games for Android.

Kafka Kube

The Kube is trapped. How? No one knows. There is only one way out...

Rotate the maze to solve kafkaesque physics puzzles. But each exit only leads to harder and harder challenges. Will you ever escape?

Dodge spikes, destroy sponge, slide past saws, collect keys and warp through portals on your journey through these fiendishly difficult puzzles.

60 puzzles, and 30 challenges, with much more on the way!

MiniChess

We all enjoy Chess: it's a timeless game.

However, in today's world there just isn't time to sit down and play a long match against a friend. But there's always time for a quick round of MiniChess!

Play Chess on a scaled down 6x6 board - it's the traditional game slimmed down and simplified! There are 4 game modes, each with different starting pieces: Mini Bishops, Mini Knights, All Pawns and Three Queens.

ARX

ARX is an exciting new Rogue-like dungeon crawler. Gird your armour, draw your daggers and drink your potions - it time to kill some monsters!

Explore an infinitely generated dungeon, collecting lavish loot and battling strange monsters. Level up to unlock new weapons and stronger creatures. Discover relaxing gardens and rare treasures. Buy exclusive items in the Dwarf's store.

Will you become the Champion of the Dungeon?

Word Fall

It's Wordle meets Tetris - and it's the BEST new word game!

Tap left and right to move the letter blocks as they fall. But don't let them reach the red line or its Game Over!

Construct words to destroy the blocks and score points. Longer words mean higher scores, so don't forget to aim big.

Some letters are hard to use: X, J, Q. Try, because if you can you will destroy a whole row!

Paint Spill

A Challenging colour-based Puzzle Game.

Choose your paint colour and then spill it on the board to make the board one single colour. Complete it in a certain number of moves to unlock the next level and do it in fewer to unlock a shiny, shiny star!

Paint Spill currently contains 8 Level Packs and an extra 2 Premium Level Packs with 320 levels in total, ranging in difficulty from Easy to Extreme.

ImpossiBall

Welcome subject 7340.

Outside mysterious beings have been wreaking havoc on the world, but in here you're safe... for the meantime. Professor Rolling has created these mazes to test the population and find the best. You must complete these challenges, the fate of the world is in your hands. Roll the ball into the hole by tilting your phone. Don't touch the walls and complete levels to progress.

There are 75+ levels to complete, and each new level pack contains brand new features which you can add to your own levels in the level builder.

Paint Spill Hex & Oct

Our Classic Game of Paint Spill but with Hexagons or Octagons and Squares.

With 200+ hexagon levels, and 200+ octagon levels. Customise your gameplay with 10 varied colour schemes.

Green Harvest

Harvest as many plants as you can before your field is overgrown. Place plants and seeds of the same type next to each other, then harvest them together.

The more plants you harvest at once, the more points you get! Each time you harvest plants, you turn areas of the soil over, creating separate fields.

If your fields become full and overgrown, it's game over!

Keeping planting and growing to discover new plants, seeds and weeds.

Building Blocks

Place Building Blocks to construct your tower. How high can you build before it falls down?

Test your skill in Timed Mode. 10 seconds on the clock, how high can you build before the time runs out? Each block you place gives you a tiny amount of extra time.

Relax and practise in Zen Mode. Take your time, there are no timers to worry about. Make sure you use this to your advantage and plan every move!

Cubes

Cube Invasion!

Tap on the cubes before you get overrun.

Unlock new cubes, by crossing new thresholds. Complete difficult challenges to test your skill to become the ultimate cube-destroyer. Check the leaderboard to compete against your friends (device only).
